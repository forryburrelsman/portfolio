Welcome to my digital portfolio where I share my accomplishments, personal and professional projects in their various stages, goals that keep me motivated, and my hopes for the future.

I'm a bootcamp graduate Software Engineer working towards my first full-time coding role. When graduating with a BS in Environmental Studies, I didn't exactly know what my future held, but I knew I wanted to do something important. On I went to work on organic farms and nonprofits in a multitude of roles. A couple years after completing undergrad, I realized I wanted more options and more experiences. Knowing I've always enjoyed problem-solving and logic riddles, I enrolled in LaunchCode's LC101 coding course. I was hooked right away and knew this is what I wanted to pursue. The possibilities in tech are truly endless!

Pivoting from sustainability and environmentalism to tech has been challenging, sometimes confusing, and a lot of fun.

Two coding courses, one cross-state relocation, and a global pandemic later and I am not giving up on becoming an employed software developer. This is only the beginning.
